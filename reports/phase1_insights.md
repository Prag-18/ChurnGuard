# Phase 1 EDA Insights

- Dataset size: 20,000 rows
- Churn rate: 25.50% (moderate class imbalance)
- Median logins: churned=16.0, retained=18.0
- Median complaints: churned=4.0, retained=2.0
- Median engagement: churned=43.0, retained=59.6
- Highest contract risk: Month-to-month (38.81% churn)
- Kaplan-Meier survival at 12 months: 83.07%

Story: Customers with low engagement, high complaints, and flexible contracts are most likely to churn, especially in early lifecycle stages.
